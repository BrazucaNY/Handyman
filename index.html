About David

With over 10 years of experience as a solo handyman, David has been serving the Long Island community with dedication and skill. His journey began with a passion for fixing things and helping people, which quickly turned into a full-time career. David prides himself on his reliability, attention to detail, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether it's a small repair or a major renovation, David approaches each project with the same level of care and professionalism.

David's expertise covers a wide range of services, from plumbing and electrical work to carpentry and painting. He is fully licensed and insured, ensuring peace of mind for all his clients. Over the years, David has built a reputation for delivering high-quality workmanship and exceptional service.

When he's not working, David enjoys spending time with his family and exploring the beautiful landscapes of Long Island. His dedication to his craft and his community is evident in every project he undertakes. Choose David for your next handyman project and experience the difference that comes with hiring a true professional.
